GenAISoc.org

A society of individuals passionate about Generative AI

Our Charter

A place for ML/AI professionals to foster communication and sharing of knowledge about GenerativeAI solutions, best practices, techniques, tools and platforms.

To provide a vendor agnostic forum with no direct marketing or member information sharing with vendors or professional service providers. Your information is kept private.

A place to discuss ethical issues regarding the use of Generative AI.

Member opportunities to help lead the community, present solutions, mentor fellow members, give back to our various local communities and provide social goods and services.
